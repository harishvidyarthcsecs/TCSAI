Blame-Free Reporting Guidelines

Know your rights. Report with confidence.

🎯 Our Commitment

OTSight Command Board is a blame-free safety and reliability reporting system. The goal is to make OT risks visible before they become incidents — not to find fault with individuals.

When you report an issue through OTSight:

  • Your report is used to improve systems and processes, not to punish individuals
  • You can report anonymously — no name will be attached
  • Even if your report turns out to be a false alarm, you did the right thing by reporting
  • Near-miss reports are valued equally to incident reports — they show the system is working

💡 What to Report

✅ Report this:

"PLC screen froze during morning shift. Restarted manually. Line stopped 5 mins."

"USB drive seen near cabinet in Zone C. Removed and given to supervisor."

"Unknown person tried to access SCADA server. Denied. Logged."

❌ Don't worry about this:

"Someone left a door open." (That's a physical security issue — not OT cyber.)

"The AC is not working." (That's facilities — not OT cyber.)
